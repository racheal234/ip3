##instructions
1.) Enter your number and ping-pong will count up to your number.
2.)ping-pong has a few exceptions

#exceptions
1.)Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with 'ping'
2.)Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with 'pong'
3.)Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with 'ping pong'

#specifications
1.)ping pong will count upto the provided input
example input(20)
example output(1,2,ping,4,pong,ping,7,8,ping,pong,11,ping,13,14,ping-pong,16,17,ping,19,pong)
